--- title: About me header: image: /assets/images/vortart/banner_vorticity.png layout: single --- I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Maryland's Atmospheric and Oceanic Science department in [Dr. Jacob Wenegrat](https://wenegrat.github.io/)'s group. My research efforts have been focused on numerical investigations of turbulent environments in geophysical boundary layers in general (both oceanic and atmospheric). More recently I began investigating submesoscale phenomena in the oceanic bottom boundary layer. For an in-depth list of talks, awards, publications, skills, etc., please check my [curriculum vitae](https://github.com/tomchor/tomchor.github.io/raw/master/assets/pdf/phd-tomas-chor3.pdf).
